## See Interhuman in action

Understand engagement, hesitation, frustration, and other [observable cues](/explanations/social-signals) across any interaction. Interhuman captures behavioral patterns and returns structured outputs your application can use to adapt responses: engagement state, social signals, and conversation quality metrics.
